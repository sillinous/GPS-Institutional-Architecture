# GPS Architecture Canvas

## Purpose
Describe the institution's intent and value creation system.

## Canvas Sections

### Purpose
Why does the institution exist?

### Actors
Who participates?

### Capabilities
What must the institution be able to do?

### Resources
What enables capabilities?

### Decisions
How are choices made?

### Outcomes
What results are created?

### Learning
How does the institution improve?

## Traceability Rule
Every capability should connect to purpose and outcomes.
