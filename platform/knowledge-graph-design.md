# GPS Knowledge Graph Design

## Purpose
Define how GPS relationships can be represented as a connected institutional knowledge graph.

## Graph Model

Nodes:
- Institution
- Capability
- Actor
- Decision
- Knowledge
- Resource
- Outcome
- Transformation

Edges:
- enables
- requires
- informs
- produces
- evolves
- measures

## Purpose
Support traceability, analysis, and AI-assisted recommendations.
