# GPS Assessment Scoring Model

## Purpose
Provide a repeatable method for evaluating institutional capability and maturity.

## Scoring Dimensions

- Capability maturity
- Decision effectiveness
- Knowledge coverage
- Outcome alignment
- Transformation readiness

## Maturity Scale

Level 1 - Initial

Level 2 - Structured

Level 3 - Managed

Level 4 - Adaptive

Level 5 - Intelligent

## Output

Scores identify improvement opportunities and transformation priorities.
