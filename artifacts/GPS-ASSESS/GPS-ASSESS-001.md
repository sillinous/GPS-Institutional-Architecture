# GPS-ASSESS-001 Institutional Assessment Framework

## Purpose
A repeatable method for evaluating institutional capability, maturity, resilience, and adaptability.

## Dimensions
- Purpose Alignment
- Capability Strength
- Decision Effectiveness
- Resource Intelligence
- Outcome Effectiveness
- Trust and Legitimacy
- Learning Capacity
- Adaptation Capacity

## Output
- Institutional Architecture Map
- Capability Health Profile
- Institutional Debt Register
- Improvement Roadmap

## Maturity
0 Undefined
1 Reactive
2 Structured
3 Managed
4 Adaptive
5 Anticipatory
6 Regenerative
